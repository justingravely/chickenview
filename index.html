Progression

All you need is exotic gear and sometimes a few masteries and you're good to go.

Gear

  • Exotic Gear
    • Soulbound Pre-BiS
    • Good enough for all content besides for high level fractals
  • Ascended Gear
    • Account Bound BiS
    • About 12.5% stronger than exotics

Specific Masteries

  • Some releated to Strikes & Fractals
  • Autoloot
  • Leyline Gliding

The rest are optional/as needed. Here is a Link to a list of ones needed for Raiding/Fractals.

Bags Are Full

Inventory management is the second worst part of the game just below no prestigious mount skins

  • Sell all junk to merchants with the sell junk button
  • Salvage everything that's yellow/green/blue
    • Basic kit -> green/blue
    • Master kit -> yellow
  • Sell exotics on the Trading Post
  • Transfer weird stuff you aren't sure about onto a bank character for later

Combat Roles

A quick look at the types of builds there are and how groups are formed

DPS

  • Power(regular) DPS
    • Better Burst DPS
  • Condi(dots) DPS
    • Better Sustained DPS

Support DPS

  • Provides one of the two major boons
    • Quickness (Bloodlust/Heroism)
    • Alacrity (Cooldown Reduction)
  • Does 60-80% DPS of a Full DPS

Healer

  • Provides one of the two major boons
    • Quickness (Bloodlust/Heroism)
    • Alacrity (Cooldown Reduction)
  • Does 0-20% DPS of a Full DPS

Five Player Team Comp

  • One Healer
  • One Support DPS
  • Fill with Full DPS
