Progression

All you need is exotic gear and sometimes a few masteries and you're good to go. A seperate set is usually needed for WvW.

Gear

  • Exotic Gear
    • Soulbound Pre-BiS
    • Good enough for all content besides for high level fractals
  • Ascended Gear
    • Account Bound BiS
    • About 12.5% stronger than exotics

Specific Masteries

  • For Strikes: Icebrood Saga -> Essence Manipulation
  • Click For Raids/Fractals List
  • Pact Commander Mastery Track for Autoloot

The rest are optional/as needed.

Gold Making

Pretty much everything earns you gold, but these are the most efficient ways that we've boiled it down to.

Daily

  • Strikes
  • Achievement Dailys
  • NPC talky grabbies
  • Daily Fish
  • Meta Event(s)

Weekly

  • Hard-Mode Strikes
  • Raids
  • Arborstone Vendor Legendary Material

Constant Grinding

  • Meta Event Trains
  • Fishing
  • Creating Legendaries to sell to rich people

Bags Are Full

Inventory management is the second worst part of the game just below no prestigious mount skins

  1. Sell all junk to merchants with the sell junk button
  2. Salvage everything that's yellow/green/blue
    • Basic kit -> green/blue
    • Master kit -> yellow
  3. Sell exotics on the Trading Post
  4. Transfer weird stuff you aren't sure about onto a bank character for later

Combat Roles

A quick look at the types of builds there are and how groups are formed

DPS

  • Power(regular) DPS
    • Better Burst DPS
  • Condi(dots) DPS
    • Better Sustained DPS

Support DPS

  • Provides one of the two major boons
    • Quickness (Bloodlust/Heroism)
    • Alacrity (Cooldown Reduction)
  • Does 60-80% DPS of a Full DPS

Healer

  • Provides one of the two major boons
    • Quickness (Bloodlust/Heroism)
    • Alacrity (Cooldown Reduction)
  • Does 0-20% DPS of a Full DPS

Five Player Team Comp

  • One Healer
  • One Support DPS
  • Fill with Full DPS
